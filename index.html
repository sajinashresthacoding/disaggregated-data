Data on different Asian-American groups exist, it's just really hard to find.

Latest Census survey contains disaggregated data on Asian Americans but finding it can often be very confusing and complicated.

By Sajina Shrestha
Dec. 05, 2022


The 2021 American Community Survey shows how data collected about Asian American groups often does not provide an accurate picture of all Asian Americans.

This is why Asian activists and groups often call for disaggregated data, data divided into smaller categories that makes it easier to see which specific communities need what.

Although disaggregated data is now available, the road to accessing it is not straightforward.

Lingying He is the Associate Director of Research at the Asian American Federation. She says that adding visual elements would greatly help the Asian community.

“Leveraging this functionality to create race/ethnicity/detailed origin-based profiles would greatly improve our community’s ability to understand the needs of the groups they serve.” said He in comments she submitted to the Census Bureau.
